Welcome

Thank you for choosing Countryside Animal Hospital. We are located at Moores Crossroads just a mile north of the American Village. Countryside Animal Hospital is a modern full-service small animal facility specializing in medicine and surgery for companion animals.

We have been at the same location since 1980 providing a unique combination of Modern Medicine and Old-Fashioned Care and Concern. In addition, we offer boarding and day care, cold laser therapy, behavioral counseling, dietary management, and dental services.

Our task is to provide excellent quality, compassionate care, and life-long health for your pet. We look forward to welcoming you and your four legged friends to our animal family. We have an exceptional staff of talented doctors and support technicians with particular interests in all aspects of animal care. Please come and tour our facility anytime. We look forward to serving you and your pets needs.

Location

Countryside Animal Hospital is located just a mile north of the American Village, near the small town of Montevallo, Alabama. We invite you to come by and pay us a visit.
